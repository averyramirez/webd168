Avery Ramirez :: Biography

I've used quite a few of the Adobe Creative products, my favorites are Photoshop, InDesign and XD. Why am I taking this course?

I began self-teaching myself Web Development almost three years ago, and while I learned a lot, I needed more structure and guidance so I decided to enroll at San Diego Mesa. Taking the first part of this course, WEBD152, taught me a lot about HTML & CSS and I'm really excited to see how much I can grow my skills in WEBD168!
